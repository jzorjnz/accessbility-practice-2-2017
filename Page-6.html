Spaghetti with Sausage Sauce
Not rated yet
By: mksasaki
Servings N/A Prep Time 20 minutes Cook Time 4 hours

Ingredients

2 lbs Pasta - Spaghetti
1/2 cup white sugar
2 tablespoons garlic salt
1 Tablespoon Garlic, minced
2 tablespoons dried parsley
1 tablespoon dried basil
1 1/2 tablespoons dried oregano
1 cup Burgundy wine
1 (16 ounce) can Tomatoes - Canned, crushed
1 can (46 fluid ounces) tomato juice
2 (6 oz) cans Tomato Paste
1/4 cup Olive Oil
2 cups sliced mushrooms
1 Onions, minced
1 lbs beef sausage
2 lbs Pasta - Spaghetti
1/2 cup white sugar
2 tablespoons garlic salt
1 Tablespoon Garlic, minced
2 tablespoons dried parsley
1 tablespoon dried basil
1 1/2 tablespoons dried oregano
1 cup Burgundy wine
1 (16 ounce) can Tomatoes - Canned, crushed
1 can (46 fluid ounces) tomato juice
2 (6 oz) cans Tomato Paste
1/4 cup Olive Oil
2 cups sliced mushrooms
1 Onions, minced
1 lbs beef sausage

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Cook sausage for 30 minutes. Cut into bite sized pieces, and set aside.

2. In a Dutch oven (you can use a pan), saute onion and mushrooms in olive oil until tender. Remove and set aside. Stir into Dutch oven: tomato paste, tomato juice, Italian tomatoes, and wine. Stir until smooth. Mix in oregano, basil, parsley, garlic, garlic salt, and sugar. Return sausage and onion and mushroom saute to sauce. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, and simmer for at least 3 hours. Cover pot if sauce becomes too thick.

3. Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain. Serve sauce over pasta.
